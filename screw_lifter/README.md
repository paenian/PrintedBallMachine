# Screw Lifter
This is a lifter module, utilizing two separate screw lifters and a spiral drop.

Mechanical Parts:
2 motors
2 M5 screws and nuts for the handle

Parts to Print:
2 * sl_inlet.stl
2 * sl_screw_2s.stl
1 * sl_bowl_drop.stl
1 * sl_bowl_catch.stl

Standard Parts
2 * stand.stl
1 * handle.stl
1 * breturn_inlet.stl
1 * breturn_outlet.stl
2 * breturn_peg.stl
4 * peg_1x.stl
2 * peg_2x.stl
4 * peg_3x.stl
1 * insert_peg.stl

Notes:
Motor direction matters!
sl_inlet uses long pegs where it can.
sl_bowl_catch.stl needs an insert peg on its outlet.
